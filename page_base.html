Kevin Czap

Cartoonist, Publisher, Designer, Comics Mom

Kevin Czap (pronounced “chap”) was raised in Northern Virginia, studied art in Cleveland, OH and is now based in Providence, RI. They* run the micropress Czap Books, endearingly referred to as “Comics Mom.” In 2016, Czap received the 2nd annual Cartoon Crossroads Columbus (CXC) Emerging Talent Award for their work and involvement in the comics community. They are also one half of the design agency Studio Skep. Their comic Fütchi Perf is coming soon from Uncivilized Books.

Taurus Sun, Sagittarrius Moon, Leo Rising, etc. Available for work, speaking engagements, friendship — kevinczap at gmail.
